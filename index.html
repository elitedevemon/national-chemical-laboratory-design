NCL's strategic Research Themes

The research themes aims to leverage the laboratory's scientific excellence and passion for translation along with key stakeholder connects to develop technologies for public, private, societal and strategic goods. It is a means of delivering products/technologies to the stakeholders. Genesis of the themes is on the basis of some of the major challenges faced by the chemical industries today, like, climate change, Air, water, land pollution, affordable healthcare, over-dependence on imports. The themes are aligned with the SDGs and the major mission mode projects.
